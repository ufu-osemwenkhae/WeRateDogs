In [4]:
#Display correlation between favorite count and retweet count
plt.figure(figsize= (9,6))
sns.regplot(x="favorite_count", y="retweet_count", data= df_merged).set(title = 'Favorite Count against Retweet Count')
Out[4]:
[Text(0.5, 1.0, 'Favorite Count against Retweet Count')]

From the scatter plot as shown above,there is a positive correlation of 0.9130 between the favorite_count and the retweet_count. It is observed that the dots are concentrated around the line from 0 to around 60000 for favorite count ,showing that the relationship between the favorite and retweet count is strong, hence the retweet counts tends to increase as the favorite count increases but as you move further ie greater than 60000 one would observe that there is a weak correlation as the dots become less concentrated and farther from the line.
